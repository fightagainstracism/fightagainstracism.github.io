How can you contribute?

Donate

You can choose to donate directly to us where we will use 100% of the funds given for helping towards ending the cycle of racism. This includes creating awareness campaigns as well as helping those who have been affected. Or, you can donate to a list of campaigns that have been started by our community.

Join

Volunteer

Join our volunteering program where you can volunteer to help out thousands of people who are affected by racism. Our community is filled with people just like you - generous and passionate who aim to make a difference in the world.

Join

Fundraise

You can help us by starting a fundraise. Just choose title, pick a photo and decide what you want to help in. Spread the word about your fundraiser and ask your friends to support. Campaigns help us to reach a wider audience. Your campaign will also be featured on our website so that we can work together in reaching the goal of the campaign faster.
